When you start mining, a small part of your computer's unused processing power is redirected toward mining a popular cryptocurrency called Monero, which is secure and private. At the end of every month, we exchange the Monero for US dollars and contribute the earnings to featured project.

100% of the currency your computer generates is used by Healthfundit to fund potentially life saving research that may otherwise not get done.

Peer-review is the gold-standard of quality in science. This means scientific experts in each field review a proporal's merit. Only those that score in the top 30th percentile receive mined funds or you can select a specific area below

Most users can expect to generate around $3 to $5 per month. It might not seem like much, but because of the revolving funding model, it adds up quickly.
